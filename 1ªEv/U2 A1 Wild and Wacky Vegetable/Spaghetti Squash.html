Spaghetti Squash: Nature's Pasta

Unlike its name, spaghetti squash isn't pasta — but when cooked, its golden strands look and feel surprisingly similar! It's low in calories, rich in nutrients, and perfect for those seeking a healthy alternative to traditional noodles.

Simple Spaghetti Squash Recipe

Ingredients: 1 spaghetti squash, olive oil, salt, pepper, and your favorite sauce.

Instructions:

  • 1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  • 2. Cut the squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds.
  • 3. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
  • 4. Place face down on a baking sheet and bake for 40 minutes.
  • 5. Use a fork to scrape the inside — you'll get beautiful spaghetti-like strands!
  • 6. Top with tomato sauce, cheese, or pesto and enjoy.
